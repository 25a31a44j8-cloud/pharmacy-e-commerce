About Wellcure

Your Trusted Healthcare Partner Since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Wellcure began with a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality healthcare accessible to every Indian household. What started as a small pharmacy in Mumbai has now grown into a trusted online healthcare platform serving millions of customers across the country.

The journey began when our founder, Dr. Aarav Mehta, a practicing physician, noticed the difficulties patients faced in getting authentic medicines and reliable health information. He envisioned a platform that would combine the convenience of online shopping with the trust and reliability of a traditional pharmacy.

Today, Wellcure stands as a beacon of trust in the healthcare space, serving over 500,000 happy customers and partnering with more than 1,000 healthcare providers. Our commitment to authenticity, quality, and customer care remains as strong as it was on day one.
